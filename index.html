Code: Scenario Sensitivity (Python)

import numpy as np

# Simple EPS sensitivity to revenue growth and margin
revenue = 2_340_000_000  # base revenue ($)
margin  = 0.214          # operating margin
shares  = 800_000_000    # diluted shares

for g in [0.00, 0.05, 0.10]:
    rev = revenue * (1 + g)
    op_income = rev * margin
    eps = (op_income * 0.75) / shares  # assume 25% tax
    print(f"Growth={g:.0%} → EPS=${eps:.2f}")

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ \mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=0}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} \]

Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM):

\[ \mathbb{E}[R_i] = R_f + \beta_i\big(\mathbb{E}[R_m] - R_f\big) \]

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR):

\[ \mathrm{CAGR} = \left(\frac{\text{Ending}}{\text{Beginning}}\right)^{\tfrac{1}{n}} - 1 \]
